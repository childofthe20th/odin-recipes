Cookies

Description

Cookies are a delicious treat that can be enjoyed any time of day. They are a sweet and satisfying snack that is perfect for sharing with friends and family. There are many different types of cookies, from classic chocolate chip to oatmeal raisin to sugar cookies. No matter what your preference, there is a cookie for everyone!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually stir into the creamed mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until lightly golden brown.
  8. Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
