Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is a family favorite. It is a layered pasta dish with meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It is a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for a family dinner.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef and onion over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes, fresh herbs, sugar, spices, and seasonings to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine the cheese and eggs. Mix well.
  5. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer with noodles, cheese mixture, and meat sauce. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used up.
  6. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
